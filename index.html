The world of Indian hip-hop has been witnessing a remarkable transformation in recent years, with artists breaking the stereotypes and showcasing their unique styles and narratives. In this cultural revolution, the hip-hop duo Seedhe Maut has emerged as a force to be reckoned with. Comprising of the talented emcees Encore ABJ and Calm, Seedhe Maut is not only changing the face of Indian hip-hop but also making a significant impact on the entire Desi music culture.
